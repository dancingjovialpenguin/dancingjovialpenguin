my current obsessions

dancing jovial penguin ♡

Hello! I'm dancing jovial penguin (any pronouns), an introverted gay demimale teenager with a passion for computer science, activism, art, and analyzing my problems! This is my personal website. (´｡• ω •｡`) ♡

A short background

I created this website on May 31st, 2025. I've had a long history of coding even as a teenager, so I've always loved crafting on the web. The next day signalling the start of Pride Month motivated me to produce something creative, so I decided I would make a personal website hosted on Neocities for free. ヾ(≧∇≦)ゞ

Site sections

I write blog posts to express myself as I spend much of my time by myself contemplating about various things. They will probably be posted here, specifically at the #blogs section.

In the meantime, I find myself diverting from one topic of interest to another that I immerse myself into, so you can find what I'm currently fixating on at the #obsessions section. These interests are often what my blog posts are centered on.

I like going by nicknames, and I've had different ones on the internet. As a huge fan of the letter J and penguins, the adjective jovial catched my eye, and I placed it within dancing penguin to describe myself despite being neither a cheerful dancer nor penguin. See my presence on other social apps at #socials.

Miscellaneous

The section-switching functionality of this website was implemented solely by CSS - the sidebar of this website, how you can navigate through it, and the consequential page changes. ヾ(｡･ω･)ｼ

The background image, which is better visible on a computer? It's something I created on Krita, a free and open source software for drawing, in about 10 minutes. I wanted some graphics on my website rather than simply colors alone. I love the rain, so I thought it would make a pleasant background image. I admit how effortless it was to draw this instance though. (◕︿◕✿)

Lastly, this website is mobile-friendly - though I think, just like probably most other websites on Neocities, it looks better on a computer. Click on the Nyan Cat to toggle its animation play state. ♡♡♡

See website source code on GitHub.
