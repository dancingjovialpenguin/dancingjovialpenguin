dancing jovial penguin ♡

Hello! I'm dancing jovial penguin (any pronouns), an introverted gay demimale teenager with a passion for computer science, activism, art, and analyzing my problems!

I started creating this website on May 31st, 2025. I've had a long history of coding even as a teenager, so I've always loved web projects. The next day being Pride Month quite motivated me to produce something creative, and so I decided I would make a personal website like this on Neocities.

I plan on creating blogs to express myself as I spend much of my time by myself thinking deeply about my life and those of others. They will probably be put here, specifically at the #blogs section.

In the meanwhile, I find myself diverting from one topic of interest to another that I immerse myself into, so you can find what I'm currently fixating on at the #obsessions section.

I like going by nicknames, and I've had lots of different ones on the internet. You can discover my presence on other social apps at #socials.

Aside from all that, I'm feeling quite proud of myself for having implemented the section-switching functionality by using solely CSS and not JavaScript. What I mean is the sidebar of this website, how you can navigate through it, and the consequential page changes. Maybe, it's already an obviously easy thing to do with CSS, but I'd never thought I could do it without any scripting.

The background image (on a computer)? It's something I created on Krita, a free and open source software for drawing, in about 10 minutes. I wanted some graphics on my website rather than simply colors alone. I love the rain, so I thought it would make a pleasant background image despite how this instance was effortless to draw.

Lastly, this website is currently a work in progress and, just like probably most other Neocities websites, looks better on a computer. Click on the Nyan Cat to toggle its animation play state. ♡ ♡ ♡
